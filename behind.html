Karmanirvana


Fighting forced marriage

Our business is run by a group of young, female entrepreneurs. We'd like to help more people access the opportunities that brought us here.
We are inredibly thankful for the all that we have learnt and experienced- building a business was one of the most challenging and rewarding things we have faced.
Unfortunately, many people don't have the same options. Women and men of all ages and backgrounds are pressured into marriage and forced to abandon their careers and ambitions.
We wanted to join the fight against this injustice, as a result Wiireless donate 50% of our profits to Karmaniravana a chairty that helps to end forced marriage and protects
those affected by its threat.
